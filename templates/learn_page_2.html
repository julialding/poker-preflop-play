Learn Poker Pre-Flop Betting

Lesson 2: Starting Hand Categories

Understanding hand strength is crucial for pre-flop decisions:

Premium Hands

Always play aggressively with these hands from any position:

  • AA, KK, QQ - The big pairs
  • AKs - Ace-King suited

Strong Hands

Consider position, but generally playable:

  • JJ, TT, 99 - Medium pairs
  • AQs, AJs, KQs - Strong suited connectors

Speculative Hands

Play more cautiously, better in late position:

  • 88-22 - Small pairs
  • KJs, QJs, JTs - Suited connectors
