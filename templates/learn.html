Learn Poker Pre-Flop Betting

Lesson 1: Table Positions

Pre-flop decisions are heavily influenced by your position at the table:

  • Early Position: Tight range – premium hands only.
  • Middle Position: Moderate range – premium and some suited connectors.
  • Late Position: Wider range – suited connectors, lower pairs, steals.
  • Small Blind / Big Blind: Defend more against steals, be cautious post-flop.

    • Example strong hands: AA, KK, QQ, AKs. Adjust range as position improves.
