Lesson 3: Pre-Flop Betting Strategies

Raising

When to raise pre-flop:

  • When you have premium hands (AA, KK, QQ, AKs)
  • When in late position with decent hands to steal blinds
  • When facing a weak player who folds too often
  • To isolate a loose player who limps too frequently

Calling

When to call pre-flop:

  • With speculative hands that play well multi-way (suited connectors, small pairs)
  • When getting good pot odds against multiple limpers
  • When your hand is too weak to raise but too strong to fold

Folding

When to fold pre-flop:

  • Weak hands in early position
  • Facing a raise with marginal hands
  • When facing a re-raise and you don't have a premium hand
  • When your stack size doesn't justify speculative play
